Rules*

Fantasy Drag Race is a game you can play throughout the season of RuPaul's Drag Race Season 9.

Select who you think will make it to the top and root for those queens all season. Additionally, each week you can pick who you think will land in the top and who will be sent lipsyncing for that episode. Correct answers get you points, letting you play against your friends. Bonus points are awarded if you log back in over the weekend and correctly judge the runway.

Here are the rules for playing Fantasy Drag race:

Who will make it to the top?

Within the first three weeks you can select the girls you think will make it to the top three, and which one of those will win. Correct top 3 gets 100 points, where correct winner gets you an additional 100 points.

Earn point each weeks

If you can correctly guess who you think will land in the top two and the bottom two, you will be awarded 25 points.

Select the Queens who you think will Slay

Select the two queens that you think will come out on top this week.

Select the Queens who will have to lipsync for their lifes.

Select the two queens that you think will be up for elimination, and ultimately who will be sent home.

Enter Selection before RPDR airs

Submissions will be closed every Friday 5PM PST. Make sure to enter your submission is in so you can earn points.

Toot and Boot the looks for bonus Points

Each week after the episode airs on Friday you can log back and and Toot and Boot the looks from the runway. If you can correctly read the queens runway looks, your earn an additional 5 points based on Raja and Raven's Toots and Boots

*Rules subject to change based on what Ru has up his sleve this season
