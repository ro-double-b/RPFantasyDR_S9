Rules*

Here are the rules for playing Fantasy Drag race

Select the Queens who you think will Slay

Select the two queens that you think will come out on top this week.

Select the Queens who will have to lipsync for their lifes.

Select the two queens that you think will be up for elimination, and ultimately who will be sent home.

Earn point each weeks

For every right selection you make you will be awarded 5 points.

Enter Selection before RPDR airs

Submissions will be closed every Friday 5PM PST. Make sure to enter your submission is in so you can earn points.

*Rules subject to change based on what Ru has up his sleve this season
